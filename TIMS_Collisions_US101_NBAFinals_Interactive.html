TIMS Collisions on US-101 During NBA Finals Game Dates

What it shows:
Geocoded collision data from UC Berkeley TIMS plotted along the US-101 corridor during the June 2022 Chase Center NBA Finals game dates.

Interactive element:
Click each collision marker to view the timestamp, primary collision factor, violation category, collision type, and severity.

Why it matters:
These records help identify whether a traffic bottleneck may have been caused by a crash rather than stadium-related travel demand.
